    <h1 class="text-center">VisSURG-AID</h1>
    Visual Surgical Artificial Intelligence for Decision Support
    <!--<div class="research-funding">Project Funding Number: XXXXXXXXX</div>-->
    Hessian Ministry of Science and Research, Arts and Culture (HMWK) 
    FCMH Experimentierräume
    <div class="research-date">Project Duration: 01.01-2026 – 31.12.2026</div>
    <p class="text-block"></p>
    <p class="text-block">
      Surgical decision-making in complex or emergency interventions is inherently high-risk and frequently influenced by the individual expertise and experience of the operating surgeon. Cognitive biases and subjective judgment errors represent a major source of preventable adverse events.
Artificial intelligence (AI)-based systems designed to provide real-time, intraoperative decision support therefore hold considerable potential for improving patient outcomes. Such systems leverage intraoperative imaging data to extract relevant features and generate clinically actionable information for surgical guidance.
The primary objective of this project is the development of a consolidated data infrastructure comprising high-quality, annotated imaging datasets. These datasets will be systematically generated and enriched based on well-defined clinical scenarios. Building upon this data foundation, domain-specific AI-driven decision support systems can be designed, implemented, and evaluated
    </p>
